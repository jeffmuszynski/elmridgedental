Meet Your Dentists

Your Doctors Are Your Neighbors.

Elm Ridge is independently owned and operated by Drs. Jeff and Kayla Muszynski — a husband-and-wife team who chose to plant roots in Killeen because they wanted to build something meaningful in their own community.

Jeff and Kayla first met at Abilene Christian University on a dental mission trip to Guatemala. They both earned their degrees from the University of Oklahoma College of Dentistry and have been serving Killeen together ever since.

Outside the office, they're raising three kids and staying connected to the same community they care for every day. When you're in the chair at Elm Ridge, you're not a chart number. You're a neighbor.

Common Questions

Dental FAQs for Killeen Families.

Do you accept new dental patients in Killeen? +

Yes. Elm Ridge Implant and Family Dentistry welcomes new patients for exams, cleanings, emergency visits, cosmetic consultations, and dental implant consultations. Call 254-699-4127 or request an appointment online to get started.

Can I get same-day emergency dental care? +

We do our best to see dental emergencies as quickly as possible, often on the same day. Call right away for tooth pain, swelling, a broken tooth, a lost crown, or a knocked-out tooth.

Do you place and restore dental implants in-house? +

Yes. Elm Ridge Implant and Family Dentistry provides implant placement and restoration in one practice, including single implants, implant bridges, implant-supported dentures, and full-arch options such as All-on-4 and All-on-6.

How much do dental implants cost? +

Dental implant cost depends on how many teeth are being replaced, whether bone grafting is needed, and the type of final restoration. A consultation gives you a clear treatment plan, timeline, and financing options before you commit.

What dental insurance do you accept? +

Elm Ridge Implant and Family Dentistry accepts many major dental insurance plans, including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, BlueCross, United Healthcare, United Concordia, Tricare, and GEHA. If you do not see your plan listed, call and our team can help check your benefits.

Do you offer financing for dental care? +

Yes. We partner with CareCredit and Cherry to offer flexible payment plans. Financing can help with implants, cosmetic dentistry, emergency treatment, and larger restorative care plans.

Where is Elm Ridge located? +

Our office is located at 2601 E Elms Rd, Killeen, TX 76542. We are open Monday through Thursday from 8am to 5pm and closed Friday through Sunday.
