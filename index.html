Dental Implants

Missing Teeth Deserve More
Than a Compromise.

Dental implants are often one of the strongest long-term options for replacing missing teeth. They usually cost more upfront than dentures or bridges, but they can be more cost-effective over time because they are designed for longevity and do not rely on reshaping neighboring teeth. Elm Ridge is one of the few general practices to place and restore implants entirely in-house, using state-of-the-art 3D imaging for precision.

Whether you're missing one tooth or comparing fixed implant teeth for a full arch, we'll walk you through realistic options, timing, cost, and long-term maintenance.

  • Implants placed and restored in-house

  • Single implants, implant bridges, full-arch dental implants, and All-on-4 or All-on-X options when appropriate

  • 3D cone beam CT imaging for precise planning and predictable outcomes

  • Flexible financing - same-day consultations welcome

Common Questions

Dentist in Killeen FAQs.

What makes Elm Ridge Implant and Family Dentistry different from other dental offices in Killeen? +

Elm Ridge Implant and Family Dentistry is a private dental office focused on relationship-based care, familiar faces, modern technology, and long-term treatment planning rather than a corporate, high-volume approach.

Do you offer dental implants in Killeen? +

Yes. We provide dental implant treatment options for patients missing one tooth, several teeth, or a full arch. Treatment is planned carefully with modern imaging and a focus on natural-looking, long-lasting results.

Can I schedule an emergency dental visit? +

Yes. If you are dealing with tooth pain, a broken tooth, swelling, or another urgent dental concern, our team can help you understand your options and get appropriate care.

Do you see families and routine dental patients? +

Yes. In addition to implants and advanced treatment, we provide cleanings, exams, fillings, crowns, cosmetic dentistry, and family dental care for patients in Killeen and nearby communities.

What comfort options do you offer during dental visits? +

Patients can enjoy music throughout the office, ceiling-mounted TVs with Netflix or Hulu, provided headphones, blankets upon request, and a warm lavender-scented towelette after their visit.
