# Elm Ridge Public Cost Ranges

Owner-approved public ranges have been added to the service pages and cost pages listed below. These are typical public ranges, not guaranteed fees or exact out-of-pocket estimates.

## Ranges Now In Use

- `/dental-implant-cost-killeen-tx`: single dental implant, $3,500-$6,500.
- `/full-arch-dental-implant-cost-killeen-tx`: fixed full-arch dental implants, $25,000-$35,000 per arch; both arches, $50,000-$70,000.
- `/snap-on-denture-cost-killeen-tx`: snap-on dentures, $8,000-$14,000 per arch.
- `/crown-cost-killeen-tx`: lab-made crown, $900-$1,600 per tooth; core buildup, $250-$500 when needed.
- `/root-canal-cost-killeen-tx`: root canal, $600-$1,200.
- `/clear-aligner-cost-killeen-tx`: clear aligners, $3,500-$5,500.
- `/emergency-dentist-cost-killeen-tx`: emergency exam, X-ray, and triage, $150-$350; treatment is separate.
- `/sleep-apnea-appliance-cost-killeen-tx`: sleep apnea oral appliance, $2,500-$3,500.

## Supporting Service Ranges

- Socket preservation bone graft: $400-$650.
- Limited sinus lift / sinus bump with implant: $500-$800.
- Complete dentures: $800-$1,800 per arch.
- Immediate dentures: $800-$1,800 per arch.
- Partial dentures: $800-$1,800 per arch.
- Simple extraction: $150-$250.
- Surgical extraction: $200-$300.
- Wisdom tooth removal: $150-$500 per tooth.
- Tooth-colored fillings: $150-$450.
- Teeth whitening with custom trays and take-home gel: $250-$500.
- Porcelain veneers: $1,200-$2,500 per tooth.

## Required Caveat

Every public cost discussion should continue to explain that out-of-pocket cost can depend on insurance plan, deductible, annual maximum, remaining benefits, treatment complexity, imaging, materials, number of teeth, sedation when applicable, grafting or additional procedures when applicable, and financing selection when applicable.

Required insurance language:

> We can estimate benefits, but final payment is determined by the insurance company.
