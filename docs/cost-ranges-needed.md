# Elm Ridge Cost Ranges Needed

This internal note lists owner-approved fee ranges needed before public cost pages should display numeric ranges. Do not publish numbers until the owner has reviewed and approved them.

## Pages

- `/dental-implant-cost-killeen-tx`: single implant, abutment/crown, extraction, bone grafting, sinus lift, temporary tooth options.
- `/full-arch-dental-implant-cost-killeen-tx`: per-arch fixed full-arch range, temporary prosthesis, final zirconia, rare/select acrylic alternative, extractions, grafting.
- `/snap-on-denture-cost-killeen-tx`: implant-retained denture range by implant count, new versus existing denture, attachment maintenance.
- `/crown-cost-killeen-tx`: lab-made crown range, buildup/core range, replacement crown considerations.
- `/root-canal-cost-killeen-tx`: anterior, premolar, and molar root canal ranges; crown-after-root-canal estimate notes.
- `/clear-aligner-cost-killeen-tx`: clear aligner treatment range by case complexity, records, refinements, retainers.
- `/emergency-dentist-cost-killeen-tx`: emergency exam, X-rays/imaging, common same-day treatment estimate ranges.
- `/sleep-apnea-appliance-cost-killeen-tx`: oral appliance range, records/scans, follow-up, medical insurance billing notes.

## Required Caveats

Every public cost page should continue to explain that out-of-pocket cost can depend on insurance plan, deductible, annual maximum, remaining benefits, treatment complexity, imaging, materials, number of teeth, sedation when applicable, grafting or additional procedures when applicable, and financing selection when applicable.

Required insurance language:

> We can estimate benefits, but final payment is determined by the insurance company.
